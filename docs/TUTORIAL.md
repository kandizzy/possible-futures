# Possible Futures — Tutorial

A walkthrough of the onboarding flow and main screens.

## 1. The Ritual

Before you start, Possible Futures asks you to commit to writing this yourself.

![The Ritual](screenshots/01-begin.png)

## 2. Ready to Begin

Three commitments checked. The button activates.

![Ready to Begin](screenshots/02-begin-ready.png)

## 3. Chapter 1: The Setup

Your name, education, and the through-line that connects your work.

![Chapter 1: The Setup](screenshots/03-ch1-empty.png)

## 4. Chapter 1: Filled In

Contact info, education, and a through-line that anchors everything.

![Chapter 1: Filled In](screenshots/04-ch1-filled.png)

## 5. Chapter 2: The Throughline

Your last three roles. The spine of your career story.

![Chapter 2: The Throughline](screenshots/05-ch2-empty.png)

## 6. Chapter 2: Three Roles

Three roles with summaries and proudest moments. Enough to draw a throughline.

![Chapter 2: Three Roles](screenshots/06-ch2-filled.png)

## 7. Chapter 3: What You're Looking For

Dream role, target companies, signal words, and your compensation floor.

![Chapter 3: What You're Looking For](screenshots/07-ch3-empty.png)

## 8. Chapter 3: Signal Words & Targets

Dream role, role tiers, signal words, and a compensation floor. This powers the scoring rubric.

![Chapter 3: Signal Words & Targets](screenshots/08-ch3-filled.png)

## 9. Chapter 4: The Playbook

Your guardrails. Red flags that lower scores, words to ban from your writing, and voice calibration.

![Chapter 4: The Playbook](screenshots/09-ch4-empty.png)

## 10. Chapter 4: Playbook Started

Red flags that lower scores, banned words for generated content, and phrases that sound like you.

![Chapter 4: Playbook Started](screenshots/10-ch4-filled.png)

## 11. Chapter 5: The Shelf of Proof

Projects that prove you can do the work. Three minimum.

![Chapter 5: The Shelf of Proof](screenshots/11-ch5-empty.png)

## 12. Chapter 5: Three Projects

Three projects with descriptions and tech stacks. The proof shelf.

![Chapter 5: Three Projects](screenshots/12-ch5-filled.png)

## 13. Resume Versions

Define as many resume versions as you need. Different roles call for different emphasis. Add, remove, or rename them.

![Resume Versions](screenshots/13-ch5-versions.png)

## 14. The Reveal

Preview your generated Book, Compass, and Playbook before publishing.

![The Reveal](screenshots/14-publish-preview.png)

## 15. The Compass Preview

Your generated Job Search Compass — the scoring rubric that evaluates every posting.

![The Compass Preview](screenshots/15-publish-compass.png)

## 16. The Playbook Preview

Your Application Playbook — resume rules, cover letter rules, banned words, and version strategies. These three documents are plain markdown. You can edit them directly to customize how Claude scores and writes for you.

![The Playbook Preview](screenshots/16-publish-playbook.png)

## 17. Published

Your first edition is set. Possible Futures is primed and ready.

![Published](screenshots/17-publish-done.png)

## 18. The Dashboard

Your index of possible futures. Every role scored against your compass, with status tracking across your pipeline.

![The Dashboard](screenshots/18-dashboard.png)

## 19. Role Detail

The full scoring breakdown for a single role. Six dimensions, a fit summary, signal words found, and the AI recommendation.

![Role Detail](screenshots/19-role-detail.png)

## 20. Evaluate a Role

Paste a job posting and let the compass score it against everything you care about.

![Evaluate a Role](screenshots/20-evaluate.png)

## 21. Discover Companies

Claude reads your compass and suggests companies likely to be hiring roles that fit.

![Discover Companies](screenshots/21-discover.png)

## 22. The Houses

Your company watchlist with pipeline status. See how many roles you've scored, applied to, and where you're interviewing.

![The Houses](screenshots/22-companies.png)

## 23. The Ledger

Every API call, its cost, and what it was for. The Federation doesn't use money, but Claude does. Track your spend in latinum or USD.

![The Ledger](screenshots/23-ledger.png)

## 24. Settings

Tune your compass, choose your AI model, and manage signal words.

![Settings](screenshots/24-settings.png)
